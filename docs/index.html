English

As morning breaks, the skies unfold,

A tale of drizzle, young and old.

Hjørring's chill begins the day,

While Copenhagen's warmth finds its way.

Aalborg's heavy drizzle falls,

A somber hue that enthralls.


The winds they howl, a gentle sigh,

As raindrops weave, a melancholy sky.

Copenhagen's drizzle, soft and grey,

Invites the heart to linger, come what may.

Aalborg's heavy drizzle, wild and free,

Beckons the soul to wander, wild and carefree.

Hjørring's breeze, a gentle lover's call,

Whispers secrets, beyond them all.


In Copenhagen's warmth, I'd love to be,

Where drizzle's gentle touch, sets the heart free.

The city's charm, a haven from the grey,

A place to find solace, on a drizzly day.

For though the rain, may fall and pour,

Copenhagen's heart, remains warm, and more.


The drizzle's melody, a symphony so fine,

Echoes through the streets, a heartbeat divine.

So let the rain, fall soft and slow,

In Copenhagen's heart, my soul would glow.
