English

As morning dawns on Denmark's shore,

Three cities wake to weather's score.

Hjørring's slight rain whispers low,

A soothing melody, the winds do blow.

Copenhagen's light drizzle dances bright,

A gentle lover's caress, a wistful sight.

Aalborg's drizzle weaves a misty spell,

A mystical veil, where secrets dwell.


The temperatures tell a tale of their own,

Hjørring's chill begins, Copenhagen's warmth is known.

Aalborg's gentle breeze, a compromise makes,

Between the two, a balance it partakes.

The winds howl loud, a chorus so grand,

In Hjørring and Aalborg, a symphony to stand.

Copenhagen's tranquil heart, a refuge from the storm,

A place to find solace, where love and peace do form.


The rain falls soft, a lullaby to sleep,

In Aalborg's drizzle, dreams and magic creep.

Hjørring's rain whispers secrets, of the sea and shore,

A melancholy tune, that only the heart can explore.

Copenhagen's light drizzle, a gentle kiss imparts,

A loving touch, that soothes the soul and calms the heart.

Tomorrow, if I had to choose a place to be,

Copenhagen's gentle heart, is where I'd long to be.


The city's warmth and love, a haven from the cold,

A place to find solace, where stories unfold.

So let the drizzle fall, and the winds do blow,

In Copenhagen's heart, I'd find my peaceful glow.
