English

As morning dawns, the drizzle falls,

A gentle hush that whispers through it all,

In Hjørring's chill, the winds do sway,

A cooler breeze that chases the day.

Copenhagen's warmth, a haven from the grey,

Beckons with promise, a brighter way,

Aalborg's midst, a balance to be found,

Between the cold and the warmth unbound.


The rain, a misty veil, that shrouds the land,

Falls softest in Copenhagen's gentle hand,

Aalborg's measure, a moderate beat,

Hjørring's drizzle, a persistent repeat,

The winds, a gentle lover's caress,

In Copenhagen, a softer tenderness,

Aalborg's breeze, a moderate sigh,

Hjørring's gusts, a wilder, more restless cry.


The differences, a nuanced, subtle play,

Between the three, a varying display,

Of temperature, of rain, of wind's sweet song,

Each location, a unique, poetic throng,

Copenhagen's warmth, a siren's call,

Beckons the heart, to leave the chill behind, and all,

Aalborg's balance, a compromise, a middle way,

Hjørring's wildness, a rugged, untamed day.


So if you seek, a gentle, warmer clime,

Copenhagen's streets, may be the place to roam in time,

For though the drizzle falls, and the winds do blow,

The city's charm, will make your heart glow,

And if you're drawn, to the wild and free,

Hjørring's rugged coast, may be the place to be,

But if you seek, a balance, a middle ground,

Aalborg's gentle mix, may be the place you've found.
